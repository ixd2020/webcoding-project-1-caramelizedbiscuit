Exhibitions

Shirin's works has led her to be part of distinguished exhibitions in many museums. Her first solo exhibition was at Franklin Furnace in New York in 1993. Since then, Neshat has been featured in solo exhibitions at the Annina Nosei Gallery, New York in 1995, Museo de Arte Moderno, Mexico City; Contemporary Arts Museum, Houston; Walker Art Center, Minneapolis (2002); Castello di Rivoli, Turin; Dallas Museum of Art (2000); Wexner Center for the Arts, Columbus; the Art Institute of Chicago; the Serpentine Gallery, London; Museo de Arte Contemporáneo de Castilla y León, León; and the Hamburger Bahnhof, Berlin (2005). Additionally, in 2008, her solo exhibition "Women Without Men" opened at the ARoS Aarhus Kunstmuseum, Denmark, and traveled to the National Museum of Contemporary Art, Athens, and to the Kulturhuset, Stockholm. major retrospective of Neshat's work, organized by the Detroit Institute of Arts, opened 2013. In 2019, The Broad Museum in Los Angeles, presented Shirin Neshat's largest exhibition internationally, 'I Will Greet the Sun Again'. Shirin has made her mark almost everywhere and it's extremely prominent of her.
