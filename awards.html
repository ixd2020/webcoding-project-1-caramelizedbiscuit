Awards

Shirin's hard work and growth as an artist has led her to her success. Throughout her journey as a contemporary artist, it has led her to win the hearts of many but also, obtained very prestigious awards such as First International Prize at the Venice Biennale in 1999. Here are more of what Shirin's hardwork and beliefs has brought her to winning...

  • First International Prize at the Venice Biennale

  • Grand Prix at the Kwangju Biennale

  • Visual Art Award from the Edinburgh International Film Festival

  • Infinity Award from the International Center of Photography, New York

  • ZeroOne Award from the Universität der Künste Berlin

  • Hiroshima Freedom Prize from the Hiroshima Museum of Art

  • The Dorothy and Lillian Gish Prize, New York

  • Rockefeller Foundation Media Arts Fellowship, New York

  • Cultural Achievement Award, Asia Society, New York

  • Silver Lion Award for Best Director, 66th Venice International Film Festival

  • Cinema for Peace Special Award, Hessischer Filmpreis, Germany

  • MilkCrystal Award, World Economic Forum, Davos, Switzerland
